<h2 style="text-align:center; margin-bottom:4px;">EXPLANATION OF RESEARCH</h2>
<p style="text-align:center; font-size:0.85em; color:#666; margin-bottom:16px;">University of Central Florida IRB &bull; IRB Number: STUDY00007380 &bull; IRB Approval Date: 7/1/2025<br>UCF HRP-254 Form v.1/31/2023</p>
<p><strong>Title of Study:</strong> Western Regional Noyce Network &ndash; Remote Professional Development Impact on Computing Engagement</p>
<p><strong>Principal Investigator:</strong> Dr. Mike Borowczak<br><strong>Other Investigator(s):</strong> Dr. Andrea C Borowczak</p>
<hr style="border:none; border-top:1px solid #ccc; margin:16px 0;">
<p>You are being invited to take part in a research study. Whether you take part is up to you.</p>
<p>The purpose of this research is to evaluate the efficacy and impact of remote professional development interventions on computing education engagement and implementation. This study specifically examines how virtual professional learning communities influence educators&rsquo; self-efficacy in computing instruction and their subsequent classroom adoption patterns.</p>
<p>You have chosen to participate in remote professional development (PD) sessions focused on computing education pedagogy and implementation strategies. <strong>The PD activities are NOT happening for research purposes.</strong> These synchronous virtual sessions will be conducted via secure video conferencing platforms (Zoom or MS Teams) and will incorporate both direct instruction and collaborative learning components.</p>
<p>During these sessions, you will:</p>
<ol><li>Engage in structured professional development activities designed to enhance computing education competencies</li><li>Participate in collaborative discussions with fellow educators</li></ol>
<p>If you agree to participate in this research study you will be asked to:</p>
<ol start="3"><li>Complete brief pre- and post-session online surveys (using Qualtrics) addressing teaching self-efficacy and implementation strategies using a link provided to you via email before and after your attendance.</li></ol>
<p>The research will be conducted entirely through virtual platforms, allowing participation from your preferred location with appropriate internet connectivity.</p>
<p><strong>Your participation in this research study involves:</strong></p>
<ul><li>Survey Completion: 10 minutes per survey with two surveys per PD; 20 minutes per PD</li></ul>
<p>You will be video and audio recorded during the PD sessions. If you do not want to be video recorded, you will still be able to be in the study. Discuss this with the researcher or a research team member. If you are recorded, the digital recording will be kept in a password protected cloud service from the meeting provider. Recordings occur using the virtual meeting platform used for the meeting (e.g., Zoom or MS Teams). The recording will be erased or destroyed five-years after study closure.</p>
<p>Since the session is virtual, your displayed meeting name along with your face and voice may be collected during the virtual meeting recording. Only the research team will have administrative access to the video/audio recording, and it will remain stored in the video-meeting provider&rsquo;s cloud-based interface for five years after study closure. The collected recordings will be analyzed by the members of the research team to assess, in aggregate and anonymized, participant engagement and discourse.</p>
<p>Your information that is collected as part of this research will not be used or distributed for future research studies, even if all of your identifiers are removed.</p>
<p>To be eligible for this study you must be 18 years of age or older and you should be or are planning to be a K-20 educator or K-20 administrator.</p>
