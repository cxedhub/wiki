We're excited for Workshop 3: Programming Edge/IoT Systems with AI on [Date]! You should have received your BBC micro:bit V2 kit by mail. Before the session, please take 10 minutes to make sure everything works.

Setup Steps (10 min)

1
Unbox your kit. You should have: 1 BBC micro:bit V2 board, 1 micro-USB cable, and 1 battery pack (2×AAA). If anything is missing, reply to this email immediately.
2
Connect the micro:bit to your computer using the USB cable. It should appear as a USB drive called "MICROBIT" in your file explorer/Finder.
3
Open MakeCode in your browser: makecode.microbit.org
4
Create a test program: Click "New Project." Drag a show string "Hi" block into the on start block. Click the purple "Download" button. Save the .hex file to the MICROBIT drive.
5
Verify: Your micro:bit should scroll "Hi" across the LED display. If it does — you're ready! 🎉
⚠️ Trouble? Common fixes:
  • MICROBIT drive doesn't appear: Try a different USB cable (some are charge-only, not data). Try a different USB port.
  • Download button doesn't work: Use Chrome or Edge (not Safari — it has pairing issues).
  • micro:bit shows "?" or error: Unplug, hold the reset button (on the back), plug back in while holding reset, then release.
  • Still stuck? Reply to this email and we'll help you troubleshoot before the session. We'll also have a Help Room breakout at the start of the workshop.
