The CRAFT Cycle

8:40 C

Contextualize

Where AI lives in STEM — and why it matters for your classroom

YOU DO Contextualize 8:45 (10 min)

Brainstorm: AI in Your World

In the shared doc, add to the table:

  • Where have you (or your students) encountered AI in a STEM context?
  • Star anything that surprises you
  • Be ready to share one highlight

→ Open Shared Doc (link in chat)

8:55 R

Reframe

AI myths vs. engineering reality

YOU DO Reframe 8:55 (3 min)

Poll: What Do You Believe About AI?

Vote honestly — no judgment here.

YOU DO Reframe 9:03 (12 min)

Breakout: Your Biggest AI Concern

In small groups:

  • What's the #1 barrier to using AI in your classroom?
  • Capture your group's top concern and one reframe in the shared doc
BREAK 9:15 (10 min)

Break #1

Check chat for a STEM dad joke.

9:25 A

Assemble

I Do → We Do → You Do: Hands-on prompt engineering

LISTEN Assemble 9:25 (10 min)

I Do: Prompt Engineering Demo

Same prompt → three platforms side by side.

  • Watch prompt anatomy: role, task, constraints, format
  • Notice cross-platform differences
  • First output = draft, not deliverable
YOU DO Assemble 9:35 (15 min)

Guided: Your First Engineered Prompt

Using the prompt template:

  • Write a prompt for YOUR subject and grade band
  • Enter it into any LLM, paste result in shared doc
  • Note: what's good, what's wrong, what's missing?

→ Open Prompt Template

YOU DO Assemble 9:50 (25 min)

Breakout: Cross-Platform Prompt Lab

Same prompt, different LLMs.

  • Compare results in your group's section
  • Prepare one 'aha' and one 'uh-oh'
YOU DO Assemble 10:20 (25 min)

Build: Customize Your Lesson Template

Use prompt engineering to populate the NGSS template for your classroom.

  • TAs circulating for support
  • Early? Try differentiation tiers or rubric generation

→ Open Lesson Template

BREAK 10:45 (10 min)

Break #2

Browse the resource website.

10:55 F

Fortify

Trust but verify — auditing AI outputs like an engineer

YOU DO Fortify 10:58 (20 min)

Spot the Difference: AI Output Audit

Call out errors in the side-by-side outputs:

  • Factual errors & hallucinated standards
  • Wrong grade levels or vague assessments
  • AI outputs are hypotheses, not answers
YOU DO Fortify 11:18 (5 min)

Quick Audit: Your Own Lesson

Find one thing the AI got wrong in your lesson template.

11:23 T

Transfer

Connect forward — your classroom, your CRAFT

YOU DO Transfer 11:28 (15 min)

Design: Your Own CRAFT Lesson Sketch

Pair up. Sketch a lesson following C→R→A→F→T.

  • Map one idea per phase
  • Share your favorite phase with the group
