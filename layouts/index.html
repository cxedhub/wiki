Our approach

Integration » addition.

We’re Drs. Andrea and Mike Borowczak. For the past 15 years we’ve been designing meaningful experiences for educators across K–12 and higher ed — work funded by the National Science Foundation, the Wyoming Department of Education, and several industry sponsors.

We know educators are already balancing full plates. So instead of piling on “one more thing,” every lesson here is built for integration, differentiation, and extension — computing woven into the subjects you already teach.

— Andrea & Mike Borowczak, University of Central Florida
