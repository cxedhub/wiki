Welcome, Educators!

Whether you’ve landed here through a recommendation or a search for quality computing-related educational resources—welcome! We’re so glad you found us.

We’re Drs. Andrea and Mike Borowczak, and for the past 15 years we’ve been designing meaningful experiences and professional learning opportunities for educators like you and student as well—across K–12 and higher education. Our work lives at the intersection of Computing + X, where X represents your core teaching area—be it language arts, math, science, social studies, or beyond.

What makes our approach unique? We understand that educators are already balancing full plates. That’s why our model is different from traditional professional development. Instead of adding “one more thing,” we focus on integration, differentiation, and extension—empowering you to embed computing concepts into your existing curriculum in ways that are sustainable, flexible, and impactful.

No matter your comfort level with computing—from complete beginner to seasoned expert—our resources are designed to meet you where you are and help you grow with confidence.

On this site, you’ll find:

  • A resource page containing:
    • Video series from previous professional development sessions, summer camps
    • Links to various materials and content
    • Upcoming PD opportunities tailored for diverse content areas and grade levels
  • A library of over 300 computing-infused lesson made by K-12 educators for K–12 educators
  • A way to connect with us to co-design custom PDs for your school or district

We invite you to explore, share, and return often—this is your space for inspiration and growth.

Cheers,
