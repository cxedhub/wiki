## Lesson Plan
### Overview
I am planning to work with Spanish speaking students and parents after school,using your resources show them what to look for when someone contacts them through email and such. I want to create Awareness and not to be afraid to ask questions.

### PRE/POST-TEST
1-In your own words, what is Cyber Security to you?
2- Are you safe?
3- How do you know you are safe?
4-How do you trust?
5-Was this project fun?
6- How can you use this information for the best and for your future?

### OBJECTIVES
1- Hoping students can learn and described vocabulary.
2- Go over the conditions of what we, teachers, are expecting from them and their behavior. 3- Keep data of the knowledge attained by the students and that the students are having fun.

### CATCH/HOOK
"Say no, not me" 
"Look for the hidden "20$" Gift "

### ACTIVITY INSTRUCTIONS
As a group we will have a discussion, after writing down the vocabulary, and finding the meaning of the vocabulary.  I will have videos that describe situations of people being hacked and what to do if it happens. I will have my groups using their own words on describing their thoughts. I am also planning to have "interviews" with trick questions to demonstrate how easy it is to be hacked.

### REVIEW
Everyone will get together at the end to discuss their opinions and outcomes.
