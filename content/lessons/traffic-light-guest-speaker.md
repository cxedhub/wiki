# Lesson Plan
## Overview
Guest Speaker Taylor McCort, District 1, Traffic Engineer.  Speaker will discuss traffic light aspects and how they relate to traffic conditions. Students will use active listening and inquiry skills to complete their KWL chart.
## ASSESSMENT PRE/POST-TEST
KWL chart - What I know, what I wonder, what I learned
## OBJECTIVES
Ask and answer questions about traffic lights and how they are programmed to be most effective for their location.

## CATCH/HOOK
Watch the video Going Green https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7nI57gjhVd8

## ACTIVITY INSTRUCTIONS
Students will write as many things as they can about what they know about traffic lights. Students will write 3 questions about what they wonder about traffic lights, during the speaking students will check off any questions that were answered and then ask any that they still wonder about.  Then will summarize what they learned about traffic lights.
