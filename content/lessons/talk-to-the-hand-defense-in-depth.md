## Lesson Plan
### Overview
Defense in depth is a strategy that leverages multiple security measures to protect an organization's assets. The thinking is that if one line of defense is compromised, additional layers exist as a backup to ensure that threats are stopped along the way.

### PRE/POST-TEST
What does Defense in Depth mean to me?
What risks are Associated with Defense in Depth not being implemented?
How can Defense in Depth be used to prevent attacks?

### OBJECTIVES
Define Defense in Depth means
Identify the risks associated with Defense in Depth not being implemented
Describe how can Defense in Depth strategies can be used to prevent attacks

### CATCH/HOOK
Take out their glove and label the appropriate area for Defense in Depth and decorate it as desired
